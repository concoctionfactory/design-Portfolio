about

Hi my name is David Lee. I studied Digital Media at the NYU Tandon School of Engineering. What is digital media you may ask? It’s a broad range of things including 3d, game design, programming, photography and other bits and bobs. Once out of school I gravitated towards 3d roles, regardless of how limited the 3d role was or it’s intended purpose. It gave me exposure to many things and different fields. As a freelancer you are required to continue learning, stay up to date, learn new programs, and fast. Somewhere along the way I realized I enjoy learning. And I started branching out. I attended graphic designing school part time and worked at a small design studio; I got to try a lot of new things as well as learn a lot at the same time. Currently, I’m continuing my learning endeavor with Front End Web. My passion for learning has given me a multi- faceted skill set with varied experience. I will continue to hone my skill set, and learn while sharing my knowledge with others.
